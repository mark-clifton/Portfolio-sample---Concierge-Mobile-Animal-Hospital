Quality Skin Care and Allergy Testing for Dogs and Cats

Just like humans, pets too can suffer from dermatological conditions and allergies. Through Dr. Jaimie’s expert knowledge and the advanced veterinary diagnostics available on our mobile unit, we are able to provide a variety of dermatological services including:

  • Skin scrapes
  • Counseling and management
  • Cytology for ear and skin infections
  • Biopsies
  • Cultures
  • Blood testing
  • Diet trials for food allergies

Skilled Diagnosis and Expert Care

Determining the root cause of many skin issues can prove difficult and frustrating, due to the fact that many dermatological conditions share similar characteristics. As a result, we will work closely with you to obtain a history of the symptoms and to explore possible diagnosis and treatment methods. In cases where follow-up or advanced care is required, Dr. Jaimie will establish a treatment plan that works for both you and your pet, and she is also happy to refer you to a specialist if advanced care is needed.

If your pet is experiencing any of the following symptoms, we recommend you schedule an appointment today.

  • Rashes
  • Dry, flaky skin
  • Excessive itching, scratching, or licking
  • Inflammation or redness
  • Round and scaly patches
  • Scabs
  • Hair thinning or loss
